# ListingProduct.vue

**Path**: `apps\front-ecommerce\components\content\ListingProduct.vue`

## Summary
This Vue component renders a product listing grid that displays e-commerce products with optional advertising placements. It handles the layout and organization of product metadata, intelligently inserting promotional ads at specified positions within the product grid, and emits tracking events when products or ads are viewed or selected. The component supports both standard product listing pages (PLP) and subscription-based product displays, while managing state for mounted status and push ad loading.
