# ShoppingPanelSubscription.vue

**Path**: `apps\front-ecommerce\components\product\ShoppingPanelSubscription.vue`

## Summary
This Vue component manages the subscription shopping panel for e-commerce products, allowing users to add subscription-based products to their draft orders. It handles variant selection (regular vs subscription), stock availability checks, quantity management, and integrates with customer subscription services to add products to the next draft order while tracking subscription-related events.
