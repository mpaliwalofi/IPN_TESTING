# SubscriptionProductCard.vue

**Path**: `apps\front-ecommerce\components\subscription\SubscriptionProductCard.vue`

## Summary
This component displays a recommended product card within a subscription management interface, allowing users to add additional products to their next draft subscription order. It handles product quantity selection (with configurable limits), validates stock availability and subscription editability, and integrates with the subscription management system to update draft orders while tracking upsell analytics.
