## Summary
This Vue component handles the second step of a bi-nutrition pet food recommendation flow in an e-commerce application. It fetches and displays personalized wet food product recommendations for cats based on pet profile data (gender, type, etc.), manages product selection with a maximum of 2 items, and tracks user progress through the bi-nutrition user guide workflow. The component integrates with catalog services, pet profile forms, and recommendation APIs to provide tailored product suggestions while handling loading states and potential errors.
