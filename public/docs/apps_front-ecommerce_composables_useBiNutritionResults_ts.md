# useBiNutritionResults.ts

**Path**: `apps\front-ecommerce\composables\useBiNutritionResults.ts`

## Summary
This composable manages the bi-nutrition (combination of dry and wet pet food) product recommendation results and pricing calculations for an e-commerce pet food store. It handles fetching recommended products based on pet form data, calculates total prices for both one-time purchases and subscriptions, and manages the process of adding recommended product bundles to the shopping cart with proper tracking integration.
