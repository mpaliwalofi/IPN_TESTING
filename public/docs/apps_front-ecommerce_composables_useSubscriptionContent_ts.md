# useSubscriptionContent.ts

**Path**: `apps\front-ecommerce\composables\useSubscriptionContent.ts`

## Summary
This composable manages subscription content display and functionality for an e-commerce application, handling both active subscriptions and draft orders. It retrieves and processes product information from both Strapi (CMS) and Sylius (e-commerce backend) systems, manages recommended products and upsell opportunities, and determines whether subscription items are editable based on the subscription state and draft order status.
