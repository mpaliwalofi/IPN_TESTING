## Summary
This Vue component implements a product metadata management system for an e-commerce storefront, specifically handling the lazy loading and state tracking of product data. The `ProductMetadata` class manages product information retrieval with three loading states (EMPTY, SUMMARY, FULL), tracks loading status and errors, and provides access to product details like SKU, slug, and reviews. It's designed to optimize product data fetching by supporting both identifier-based lookups (SKU or slug) and progressive loading of product information.
