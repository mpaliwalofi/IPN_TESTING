This Twig template renders customer information in a grid field, displaying the customer's full name in bold text followed by their email address in a paragraph below. It's designed as a reusable component for displaying customer details in admin or data grid interfaces, typically used in e-commerce or CRM systems where customer records need to be shown in tabular format.
