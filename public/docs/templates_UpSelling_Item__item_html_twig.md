# _item.html.twig

**Path**: `templates\UpSelling\Item\_item.html.twig`

## Summary
This Twig template renders a single row in an up-selling product table, displaying a product variant's promotional details including start/end dates, discount amount (either as percentage or fixed EUR amount), product/variant name, and stock status. It's designed to show merchandisers or administrators which products are being offered as up-sell opportunities with their associated pricing incentives and availability.
