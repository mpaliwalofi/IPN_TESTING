# _contact.html.twig

**Path**: `templates\bundles\SyliusAdminBundle\Customer\Show\Details\_contact.html.twig`

## Summary
This Twig template renders a customer contact section displaying the customer's email address as a clickable mailto link. It's part of the Sylius admin bundle's customer detail view, allowing administrators to quickly initiate email communication with a customer by clicking the displayed email address with an envelope icon.
