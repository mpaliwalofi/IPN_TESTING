# _details.html.twig

**Path**: `templates\bundles\SyliusAdminBundle\Product\Tab\_details.html.twig`

## Summary
This Twig template renders the "details" tab in the Sylius e-commerce admin panel for product management. It displays product configuration fields including code, enabled status, channel assignments, and product options/variants, with conditional rendering based on whether the product is simple (single variant) or configurable (multiple variants). For simple products, it additionally includes pricing information by channel and shipping requirements, while configurable products show variant selection methods and product option configurations.
