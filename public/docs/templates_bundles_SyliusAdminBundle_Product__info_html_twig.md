# _info.html.twig

**Path**: `templates\bundles\SyliusAdminBundle\Product\_info.html.twig`

## Summary
This Twig template renders product information in the Sylius admin panel, displaying a product's thumbnail image, name, variant code, and optional logistic code. It also conditionally shows product options/variant details and any upsell pricing adjustments when viewing order items, providing administrators with a comprehensive view of product variants within orders or product listings.
