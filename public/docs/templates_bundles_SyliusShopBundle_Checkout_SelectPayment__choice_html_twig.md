# _choice.html.twig

**Path**: `templates\bundles\SyliusShopBundle\Checkout\SelectPayment\_choice.html.twig`

## Summary
This Twig template renders a single payment method option in the Sylius e-commerce checkout flow. It displays a radio button for selecting the payment method along with its label and optional description, and includes special handling to render PayPal-specific buttons when the payment gateway is configured as PayPal. The template is part of the payment selection step during checkout, allowing customers to choose their preferred payment method.
